# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist for College Students

Do’s:
 ✔ Think before you post and ensure your content is appropriate.
 ✔ Maintain privacy by adjusting your account settings.
 ✔ Use respectful and polite language in all interactions.
 ✔ Share useful, positive, and informative content.
 ✔ Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading fake news.

Don’ts:

 ✘ Do not share personal or sensitive information publicly.
 ✘ Avoid posting offensive, abusive, or harmful content.
 ✘ Do not engage in cyberbullying or online arguments.
 ✘ Avoid accepting friend requests from unknown people.
 ✘ Do not spend excessive time on social media affecting your studies.
