# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist (For College Students in India)

* ✔ Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts (combine letters, numbers, and symbols).
* ✔ Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on email, social media, and banking apps.
* ✔ Do not click on suspicious links or download unknown attachments from emails or messages.
* ✔ Verify the authenticity of websites before entering personal information (check “https://” and domain name).
* ✔ Avoid using public Wi-Fi for sensitive activities like online banking or payments.
* ✔ Keep your devices and apps updated to protect against security vulnerabilities.

**Financial / UPI Safety Tips:**

* ✔ Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or bank details with anyone—even if they claim to be from a bank.
* ✔ Double-check the receiver’s name and UPI ID before making any payment.
* ✔ Do not approve unknown “collect requests” in UPI apps.

**Reporting & Help:**

* ✔ Report cyber fraud immediately at the National Cyber Crime Portal: cybercrime.gov.in
* ✔ Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 for quick assistance

This checklist helps students stay aware and protected while using digital platforms.
